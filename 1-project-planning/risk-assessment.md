## 1. Risk Assessment Matrix

**Likelihood:** 1 (Rare) → 5 (Almost Certain)
**Impact:** 1 (Negligible) → 5 (Critical)
**Risk Score:** Likelihood × Impact

| Score | Level |
|-------|-------|
| 1–4 | Low |
| 5–9 | Medium |
| 10–16 | High |
| 17–25 | Critical |

## 3. Mitigation Plans

| ID | Risk | Mitigation Strategy | Contingency | Owner |
|----|------|--------------------|-----------|----|
| R01 | Team member unavailable | Schedule simulation 2 days before deadline; document all steps so others can step in | Reschedule simulation; reduce scenario scope | IR Lead |
| R02 | Scope creep | Maintain a strict scope document; reject out-of-scope requests | Freeze scope at end of Week 1 | Role 3 |
| R03 | Missed architecture deadline | Set internal deadline 2 days before actual; daily check-in in Week 2 | Role 1 assists Role 2 with architecture | Role 2 |
| R04 | Poor communication | Weekly WhatsApp group update; shared task board | Escalate to team lead for resolution | All |
| R05 | GitHub not maintained | Each member commits their work daily; weekly repo review | Role 3 takes over repo management | Role 3 |
| R06 | Complex simulation | Simplify to tabletop exercise; pre-define all stages in script | Reduce attack stages; use documented log | Role 2 |
| R07 | Incomplete documentation | Role 3 reviews all technical docs before submission | Add documentation review checkpoint in Week 3 | Role 3 |
| R08 | File loss | All files stored on GitHub AND Google Drive backup | Recreate from notes within 1 day | All |
| R09 | Tools unavailable | Use free alternatives (Markdown, draw.io); pre-install all tools | Use manual diagrams in ASCII format | Role 2 |
| R10 | IR Plan gaps | Treat gaps as findings — document in lessons learned | Update IR plan immediately post-simulation | Role 3 |
| R11 | DEPI guidelines not met | Map every deliverable against DEPI checklist weekly | Full review against guidelines in Week 4 | Role 3 |
| R12 | Academic integrity | All sources cited; original analysis and writing | Use plagiarism check before submission | Role 1 |
| R13 | Poor presentation | Full rehearsal on Week 4 Day 4 with all members | Each member prepares individual section notes | Role 4 |
| R14 | Grader unfamiliarity | Include a glossary; explain all technical terms | Add executive summary accessible to non-technical reader | Role 3 |
